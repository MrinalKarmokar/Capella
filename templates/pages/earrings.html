About Earrings


Through the years among women, earrings are a symbol of womanhood and a sign of identification. There are various styles of earrings available, these types are given below. Earrings can be made from metals like gold and silver as well as designed with diamonds. Here at Capella you design your own earring as it represents yourself and is unique in its own way and we make it exactly according to your design as well as your budget.Select your components, submit your design for it and you are good to go.

Earring Styles


Stud

Studs are often built around an earring casing which can be pounded or shaped from a precious metal like gold or silver and then fitted with some kind of gemstones that serves as central ornament to the stud.

Cluster

Cluster earrings are types of earrings with crystals or gemstones all grouped together.

Jackets

Jacket is an extra component that goes along with your existing earring post or stud, the jacket is designed to wrap around your stud earrings and tend to add sparkle and beauty.

Drop

Drop earrings are those that "drop" just below the earlobe drop earrings are simple pieces with attachments like gemstones, beads or charms dropping from the base of the earring.

Dangle

Dangle earrings are those that hang and swing to and fro or from side to side. Dangle earring typically hangs below the earlobe and move.

Chandelier

Chandelier type of earrings are made from a combination of beads or jewels connected with metal, hanging freely in a cascading shape.

Hoops

Hoops earrings are a staple in earring collection and these can be made in a large variety of sizes.

Hinged/Huggie

Hinged/Huggie are a style of little hoop earring that "hug" the earlobe whether they are simply silver, gold or rosegold plated huggies.

Ear Trim

Ear Trim earrings are placed or fixed onto the earlobe.
