About Rings


Rings are emblem of companionship through time, a symbol of devotion and an agreement between parties. There are various styles of rings available, these types are given below. Rings can be made from metals like gold and silver as well as designed with diamonds. Here at Capella you design your own ring as it represents yourself and is unique in its own way and we make it exactly according to your design as well as your budget.Select your components, submit your design for it and you are good to go.

Ring Styles


FASHION

Fashion rings are worn partly for fashion or styling purposes.

Halo

Halo rings have a setting that encircles a center gemstone in a collection of round pave or micro pave diamonds(or gemstones).

3 - Stone

Three stone rings are set of three gemstones or mixed colour gemstones.

Promise

Promise rings can be made of any metal and incorporate any gemstone.

Semi-mount

Semi-mount rings has mounting that are already set with melee or baguette diamonds or coloured gemstones.

AWARD/SIGNET

Award/Signet rings have letters or design carved on it. It is a ring decorated with special design.

Engagement

Engagement rings consist of atleast one diamond or valuable gemstone set upon a quality metal such as gold and platinum.

SOLITAIRE

Solitaire rings will have a single diamond rock mounted on a jewellery set.

Bridal-set

Bridal-set consists of engagement ring and matching band for the bride.The set features two rings that usually match in style or colour.

wedding band

Wedding bands are traditionally a plain metal band or diamond encrusted eternity band.

ANNIVERSARY BAND

Anniversary bands can be made of plain metal with some design on it or encrusted with a diamond.

ETERNITY BAND

Eternity bands comprise of precious metal(usually gold) set with a continuous line of identically cut gemstones.
