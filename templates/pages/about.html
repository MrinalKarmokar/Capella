Our Story


Who are we

Capella was founded in 2021 with an aim of creating beautiful jewellery which our customers design for themselves. Our motto is 'You design, We create'.

As we surveyed that people are not satisfied while buying jewellery as many times because they don't get the design which they want so they need to settle with similar or different designs. Capella was founded to give people access to fine jewellery which is designed by themselves.
