About Neckwear


Necklaces may have been one of the earliest types of adornment worn by humans.There are various styles of neckwear available, these types are given below. Neckwear can be made from metals like gold and silver as well as designed with diamonds. Here at Capella you design your own neckwear as it represents yourself and is unique in its own way and we make it exactly according to your design as well as your budget.Select your components, submit your design for it and you are good to go.

Neckwear Styles

Riviera

Riviera is a necklace that is comprised of gemstones of the same species that are all the same size and shape or graduate smoothly in size.They are usually designed without any ornamentation allowing the gemstones to be featured.

Chain

Chain resembles a cable in its design.It is technically a type of necklace.

Pendant

Pendant is a loose-hanging piece of jewellery, generally attached by a small loop to a necklace.

Station

Station necklace is exactly how it sounds. Adornments sit along a chain in station be they gemstones, beads or even an assortement of shapes.

Lariat

Lariat necklace is a long line or string of materials usually measuring between 30 to 50 inches without a clasp or closure on either end.Each end of the length is often completed with a decorative element such as a drop or tassel.

Collar

Collar necklace is not to be confused with a choker necklace.Collar necklace sit flush against the skin and rest directly above the collar bone.

Choker

Choker type of necklace fit tight to the neck.

Bail styles

Standard

Standard bails have a drop ring to connect to your pendant and then the main bail loop slides onto your necklace or chain.

Rabbit Ear

Rabbit Ear bails are designed according to the shape of rabbit ears consisting of two joint bail loops.

Decorative

Decorative bail styles consist of different shapes.


Pendant styles

With bail

Pendant which is made with a bail connected to it.

Solitaire

Solitaire pendant is a necklace with one gemstone usually diamond.

Slide

Slides can be pendants with an open center that a chain can be slid through, a tube-shaped component that is incorporated into the design or attached to the back, a couple of bars (kind of like belt loops) on the back of the piece, or an extra large bail that slides over the chain.

Hidden Bail

A hidden bail is typically connected to the back of a pendant in such a way that the chain can pass through and support the pendant, but the bail cannot be seen.

Locket

Locket are types which come in different shapes and sizes.

Roundel

Roundel is a rounded shaped pendant style.
