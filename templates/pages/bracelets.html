About Bracelets


A bracelet is best known as a symbol of a relationship to someone. There are various styles of bracelets available, these types are given below. Bracelets can be made from metals like gold and silver as well as designed with diamonds. Here at Capella you design your own ring as it represents yourself and is unique in its own way and we make it exactly according to your design as well as your budget. Select your components, submit your design for it and you are good to go.

Bracelet Styles


Line/Tennis

Line/Tennis type of bracelets of individual, flexible, links which are set with gemstones of the same size.

Link

Link type of bracelets have chained design, smaller components of the overall structure are linked together or chained together.

Cuff

Cuff type of bracelets are piece of jewellery that goes around the wrist as decoration(cuff means that the bracelet is wide or thick or both).

Bangle

Bangles are inflexible but loose fitting bracelets, generally made out of some hard material like gold, silver, wood, plastic or ivory.
